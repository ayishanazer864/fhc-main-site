About Us

FISAT HORIZON CLUB is the official technology and innovation club of FISAT Engineering College, initiated by IEDC FISAT in collaboration with the Computer Science department. As a recognized student organization, we are dedicated to merging the worlds of virtual reality and innovative design, providing our members with official academic support and industry-standard resources to excel in the field of technology.

Our Mission

To foster a dynamic learning environment where students can explore and master the latest advancements in technology. Through hands-on projects, workshops, and collaborative challenges, we empower our members to create meaningful impact in the field.

Our Vision

To be a leading technology development club recognized for advancing knowledge and skills in computer science, creating a community of tech enthusiasts who drive technological progress and make a positive impact on the industry.
